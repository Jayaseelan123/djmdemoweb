FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking at least 6–12 months in advance for weddings and large events. For corporate events and parties, 2–3 months is typically sufficient. However, we do accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

Do you offer event packages or custom pricing? +

We offer both curated packages and fully custom pricing. Every event is unique, so we tailor our services and pricing to match your specific needs and budget. Contact us for a free consultation and detailed quote.

What is your cancellation policy? +

Our cancellation policy varies by event type and time of cancellation. Generally, bookings cancelled 90+ days before the event receive a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations within 30–90 days may incur a partial fee. Full details are provided in our contract.

Do you handle destination events? +

Absolutely! We have planned events across the United States and internationally. Our team has the experience and vendor network to execute flawless events regardless of location.
